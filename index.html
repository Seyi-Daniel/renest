Buying used & new furniture on Renest is simple

Browse

Browse used & new furniture listings by category or brand.

Request to Purchase

Reserve the product before someone else gets to it! Your credit card will only be charged when the seller confirms within 24 hours of your purchase request.

Schedule Delivery

Select a delivery date that works for you then sit back and relax - we’ll arrange the rest. Our delivery team will verify the condition of your items at time of pickup & deliver on the date/time you selected. You can also select to pick it up yourself.

Selling furniture on Renest is effortless

List it

Post your furniture - it’s supereasy & takes couple of minutes. it’s free to post and we will only deduct a small fee once it sells.

Confirm Purchase request

An interested buyer will submit a request to purchase your product(s). Confirm the request within 24 hours to avoid cancel option.

Pickup/Delivery

Choose pickup date(s) that work for you. Our delivery team will pickup your product(s)and deliver to the buyer. If the buyer decides to pickup the item(s) themselves you can co-ordinate a date & time directly with them.

Get paid

Payment is processed at the time of pickup & deposits to your account within 24 - 48 hours. It’s totally simple, safe and secure! That’s it!
